Our story

Amindset removes the barriers so everyone can instantly connect with a healthcare provider. We are a team of innovators, strategists and developers that came together to make it easy to meet with someone you can talk to on a secure platform. We make it simple, safe and fun.

Why Amindset

1
Quality

Our providers are licensed and certified. They have a reliable history of success and satisfaction with their clients. Quality care and associated services are our primary standard that is upheld by all providers in the platform.

2
Privacy

We are a HIPAA compliant platform guided by private and confidential standard and procedures. Our system offers encrypted and secure channels of communication between you and your health care provider. Personal information is kept confidential and is not shared with any third party apart from you and your healthcare provider.

Affordable

Our consultation services are free. This allows you to choose the health care provider that is best for you without worrying about consultation fees.
